Our Time Machine

Click a year to view

More Flow

Festival Dates 26.09 — 01.10.2023
Creative Director Eduardo Cachucho

Twelve editions. Twelve years of public experimentation with African digital culture, documented, indexed, and held in the Tshimologong archive. The Time Machine is a structured record of what Fak'ugesi has built, tested, exhibited, and released since its founding in 2014.

The archive is is in the process of being built. Programme records, participant listings, award recipients, partnership histories, and documented outcomes are being collated. Not as a highlights reel but as evidence of a practice that has been running, iterating, and compounding for twelve years.

Twelve years of African digital practice, held in one place.
