Tshimologong

The Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct is a purpose-built environment adjacent to the campus of Wits University in Johannesburg. Designed to accelerate the conditions under which African digital innovation occurs, scales, and circulates, the Precinct houses research groups, start-up incubators, creative studios, and industry partnerships inside a shared innovation environment.

Its mandate is practical: reduce the distance between discovery and deployment, between the laboratory and the market, between the individual practitioner and the structural support they need to build something that lasts.

During Fak'ugesi, the Precinct becomes the physical centre of gravity for the festival's five days. A place where the African Imaginaries are not only discussed but built, tested, exhibited, and released into the world.
