FAQ

Dates, access, and programme information for Fak'ugesi 2026.

Fak'ugesi 2026 runs from 13 to 17 October at the Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct, Johannesburg.
The programme is organised across three Frequencies — Memory Engines, Imagination Engines, and Future Engines — running concurrently across the five days.
Some programme elements are open access. Others require registration or ticketing. Full details are published with the festival programme. Visit the Tickets page for current information.
The Tshimologong Precinct is wheelchair accessible. Full accessibility information will be published on the Visit page ahead of the festival. For specific requirements, contact hello@fakugesi.co.za in advance.

Our Main Partners

View all partners →

Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct is the home of Fak'ugesi and a hub for Africa's digital creative and technology ecosystem.

The University of the Witwatersrand provides the academic and research infrastructure underpinning the Fak'ugesi festival and its programmes.

The Wits Anglo American Digital Dome is Africa's premier immersive environment, hosting the Immersive Africa strand of the festival.
