FAQ

Everything you need to know about exhibiting at and attending the Fak'ugesi Market.

The Market is open to digital creative practitioners, collectives, studios, and small ventures presenting work, products, or services that connect to the festival's Frequencies. Contact hello@fakugesi.co.za for exhibitor enquiries.
Applications are open through the Get Involved section. Exhibitor enquiries can also be directed to hello@fakugesi.co.za.
Exhibitor pricing and terms will be confirmed with your application. Contact hello@fakugesi.co.za for a current rate card.
The Market takes place at the Tshimologong Digital Innovation Precinct, 41 Juta Street, Braamfontein, Johannesburg, across all five days of the festival.
